Here’s a strong set of **questions you can ask an interviewer** — grouped by theme so you can pick the ones most relevant to your situation:

---

## 🏢 Role & Responsibilities
- What does a typical day look like for someone in this role?  
- What are the immediate priorities for the first 3–6 months?  
- How does this role contribute to the company’s overall goals?  
- What challenges do you expect the person in this role to face?  

---

## 👥 Team & Collaboration
- Can you tell me about the team I’d be working with?  
- How does the team handle cross-functional collaboration (e.g., DevOps, QA, Product)?  
- What’s the management style of the team lead or manager?  
- How are decisions typically made within the team?  

---

## 🚀 Growth & Development
- What opportunities are there for professional development and learning?  
- How does the company support career progression?  
- Are there mentorship or knowledge-sharing programs in place?  
- How do you measure success and performance in this role?  

---

## 🛠 Technical & Process
- What technologies or frameworks are most critical to success here?  
- How mature are your CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices?  
- How do you approach system design trade-offs (e.g., scalability vs simplicity)?  
- What’s the process for handling technical debt or refactoring?  

---

## 🌐 Company Culture & Vision
- How would you describe the company culture?  
- What values are most important to the organization?  
- How does leadership communicate vision and strategy to the teams?  
- How does the company balance innovation with stability?  

---

## 📊 Future & Strategy
- What are the company’s biggest goals for the next year?  
- How does this role evolve as the company grows?  
- Are there upcoming projects or initiatives that this role will be heavily involved in?  
- How does the company stay competitive in its industry?  

---

✅ **Pro tip:** Always end with something like:  
- “What do you enjoy most about working here?”  
- “Is there anything about my background you’d like me to expand on?”

### 3️⃣ Code & Design Standards

### 4️⃣ Testing & DevOps
